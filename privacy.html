Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Kaito Shiro ("we", "our", "us") handles information in connection with our educational video content platform and related services ("Service").

2. Information We Collect

Our Service primarily distributes educational video content through third-party social media platforms. We may collect:

  • Platform interaction data: Publicly available engagement metrics (views, likes, comments) provided by social media platforms
  • OAuth tokens: When users authorize our application to post content on their behalf, we store authentication tokens securely
  • Contact information: If you contact us directly, we may collect your name and email address

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Publish educational video content to social media platforms
  • Analyze content performance to improve our educational materials
  • Respond to inquiries and feedback

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With social media platforms as required for content publishing (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram)
  • When required by law or legal process

5. Third-Party Services

Our content is distributed through third-party platforms including YouTube (Google), TikTok (ByteDance), and Instagram (Meta). Each platform has its own privacy policy governing data collection and use:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information under our control. API credentials and authentication tokens are stored securely and are not exposed publicly.

7. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Authentication tokens are refreshed periodically and old tokens are deleted.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Revoke any OAuth authorizations through the respective platform settings

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us through our social media channels @KaitoShiro.
